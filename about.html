PRIME MARINE SAFETY & EQUIPMENT Sarl, is a newly created subsidiary; marine safety, inspection, fire fighting and marine products company.

We sell and install smoke detectors, heat detectors and fire alarm systems. We sell service, and we also re-certify all types of portable fire extinguishers, fire pumps, water piping installations, fire fighting and on-shore supplies of offshore safety equipment / personal protective equipment such as: life jackets, offshore personnel baskets and others. We also supply pressure hoses of all sizes/notes, marine paints and chemicals, marine ropes of all sizes, paddles and other accessory materials such as (Flags, valves, fittings, bearing clamps).

Prime Marine Safety & Equipment is an internationally accredited safety and engineering company. At PRIME MARINE SAFETY & EQUIPMENT, we deal with onshore/offshore safety equipment: Maintenance, re-certification and mechanical and electrical repair of equipment. We do business in the Gulf of GUINEA and other parts of AFRICA; our intentions and missions for the creation or establishment of PRIME MARINE SAFETY & EQUIPMENT are as follows:

  • Open up more options for cost-efficient products, professional services and quality products.
  • Creating solutions to the urgent needs of onshore/offshore safety materials, production companies in the oil and gas sector, maritime industries, as well as other equipment manufacturing industries in this region.
  • Creating more channels of product knowledge and awareness for the operating industries, introducing new products and international regulations adopted for the safety of personnel working in hazardous areas.
