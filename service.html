Load & Pressure Testing

Our testing solutions replicate real-world conditions to validate performance and durability under maximum stress. This reduces potential hazards and confirms readiness for demanding applications.

+
What we deliver
+
When you need us
✕

What we deliver

- Conduct proof load and functional testing of gangways, pilot ladders, and cranes.

- Perform hydrostatic and pneumatic leak testing of hoses and pipelines using controlled procedures, with NDT as required.

- Provide corrective actions including hose/wire replacement, seal renewal, and structural reinforcement.

- Document all results with photos, videos, load charts, and pressure logs for commissioning and statutory records.

When you need us

- During routine maintenance cycles or class survey preparations (SOLAS Reg. II-1/3-9, MSC.1/Circ.1331)on detection of anomalies.

- Before transferring hazardous cargo, chemicals, or fuel-where hose and pipeline integrity is critical.

- Ahead of cargo operations, crew embarkation, or pilot boarding to ensure safe access.
