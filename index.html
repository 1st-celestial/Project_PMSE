ABOUT US

PRIME MARINE SAFETY & EQUIPMENT is a leading provider of marine and onshore safety solutions, inspections, fire-fighting systems, and technical services.

At Prime Marine Safety and Equipment, we are dedicated to delivering world-class safety solutions and technical services to both marine and onshore industries. Established in 2019, in just six years, we've gained remarkable traction, forging key partnerships, and earning recognition as one of the fastest‑growing firms in our field. Driven by a culture of innovation, excellence, and customer-centric service, we continue to scale rapidly while delivering measurable value and impact to our clients, aiming to achieve maximum customer satisfaction.
Our team has undergone extensive training with original equipment manufacturers such as Bristol, Lalizas, etc and holds accreditations from Class Burea Veritas, CCS, Lloyd's Register, DNV, and ABS. Operating across two countries, with our headquarters in Pointe-Noire and a branch in Cotonou, we are committed to protecting lives and property on land and at sea. By implementing proactive systems and rigorous safeguards we empower industries and workers in hazardous environments to operate without compromise.

OUR SERVICES

At PRIME MARINE SAFETY & EQUIPMENT, we are dedicated to delivering comprehensive services, helping our clients maintain operational integrity and regulatory compliance.

Certification & Recertification

We provide certification and recertification of safety, fire-fighting, and lifesaving equipment in line with IMO, SOLAS, ABS, DNV, and other class society requirements. Our engineers ensure compliance and safety at sea.

Inspection & Maintenance

Regular inspections and preventive maintenance to extend the shell life of marine and industrial systems, we adhere to the necessary regulations to keep all equipment in operational readiness.

Callibration & Automation Services

We provide calibration of sensors, meters, alarms, hazard detectors, and automated safety systems using Standardized instruments to ensure accuracy and conformity with ISO, IEC, and flag state standards.

Load & Pressure testing

Our team conducts proof load and hydrostatic pressure tests on lifting appliances, tanks, cylinders, and fire hoses to verify strength and adherence to international safety standards.

Procurement of Equipments

We source and supply genuine, approved industrial and marine FFE, LSA, PPE, and operational tools from trusted global brands, ensuring quality and meeting international requirements.

Navigation & Communication Systems

We install, test, and service GMDSS, AIS, ECDIS, radar, and gyrocompass systems, ensuring vessels meet SOLAS and IMO standards for safe navigation
